About

Yiming Luo 罗亦鸣

I am a Ph.D candidate under the supervision of Prof. Hai-Ning Liang from School of Advanced Technology, Xi'an Jiaotong Liverpool University (XJTLU). I received B.Eng degree from Electric & Electronic Engineering, University of Liverpool (2018); I received M.Sc degree from System Control & Signal Processing, University of Southampton. I am now working in the X-CHI lab at XJTLU. His research interests focus on robotics, human-robot interaction, teleoperation and virtual reality. My research goal is to make the interface between humans and robots more humanized and more aligned with user needs, the interaction process more natural, and the management and display smoother. From the human point of view, use user-centered design to design an interface that can improve user experience. The medium of the interface includes, but is not limited to, virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), and mixed reality (MR). Here is my CV.
